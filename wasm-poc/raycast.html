Raycast playground:

Input:

  • Drag on the background to initiate a raycast (up to the line length).
  • Drag & drop entities to move them.
  • Hold shift while dragging entities up/down to rotate them.

Output:

  • First object hit by ray will be given red outline.
  • For all objects hit (up to the line length) the intersection point will be marked with a purple dot.
